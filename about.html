About Us

Welcome to the Cybersecurity Research Lab at the Department of Computer Applications, Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT). Our lab is dedicated to advancing the field of cybersecurity through cutting-edge research and practical solutions.

Key Facilities and Tools

  • FRED: High-end forensic workstation for advanced investigations.
  • VPER Kit: All-in-one field cyber forensic kit for on-site analysis.
  • ULTRA Kit: Write blockers and forensic duplicator for preserving data integrity.
  • Universal Forensic Extraction Kit: Versatile tool for evidence extraction.
  • Amped Authenticate: Software for digital image verification.
  • Encase V7: Leading software for cyber forensic analysis.
  • FTK-6: Comprehensive forensic toolkit for digital evidence examination.
